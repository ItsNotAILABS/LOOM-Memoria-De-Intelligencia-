## I. The Problem With Stored Memory

- Deleted (by remove)

The analogy from physics: a gravitational field exists everywhere a mass influences spacetime. You cannot "delete the field" by deleting coordinates — the field is not *at* coordinates, it is the *effect of* the mass. Phantom memory works the same way. The intelligence is not stored — it is the ongoing effect of the being's frequency presence.
